WHAT IS PIXEL PROPERTY?

PixelProperty is a digital art canvas integrated with blockchain technology. The digital canvas is fully community-driven, powered by people coming together to play, create, and share. Drawing and sharing content is free for everyone, however, Ethereum users are rewarded PixelPropertyToken (PXL).

The canvas is structured as 10,000 regions; cryptocollectables known as Properties. Properties can be traded with other users for PXL. Property owners are given exclusive advertising and PXL features for that region.
