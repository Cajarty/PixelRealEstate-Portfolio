FAQ

WHAT IS PIXEL PROPERTY?

Pixel Property is a decentralized application which powers an evolving digital canvas; where the evolution is powered by the users. Pixel Property is made up of digital Properties which are tradable cryptocollectable tokens powered by Ethereum. The properties are powered by PixelPropertyToken (PXL), the underlying ERC20 cryptocurrency of PixelProperty.

WHAT IS PXL?

PXL is the utility token of PixelProperty. It is rewarded to users based on a number of factors, primarirly based around contributing to the evolving canvas. PXL can be spent within the ecosystem to access extra features such as allowing Propety owners to better advertise their displays.

WHAT IS A PROPERTY?

The evolving canvas is split up into 10,000 small segments known as Properties. These Properties are a small canvas in themselves and can be modified by users to advertise their websites or share messages. Properties are tradable cryptocollectable tokens.

IS IT FREE?

Yes! PixelProperty is available to everyone, now installation or account registration required. Free users have the ability to express themselves on the canvas through drawing and sharing links. Those who choose to sign up via MetaMask will be able to earn rewards and have access to some extra features.

HOW CAN I PARTICIPATE?

PixelProperty is a DApp powered by Ethereum, which users can interact with in a way very similar to CryptoKitties.
Anyone can draw and uplod to the canvas. However, to earn rewards, you must download the MetaMask browser extension, have some ETH, and head over to the Canvas. The Canvas page will let you know if you missed a step.

WHAT DO I NEED TO USE PIXELPROPERTY?

  • A computer runnning Brave, FireFox, or Google Chrome
  • Optional • MetaMask browser extension installed
  • Optional • Ether (ETH)

HOW DO I GET ETHER (ETH)?

For U.S. citizens, ETH can be bought within MetaMask itself.

For non U.S. citizens, you will have to buy it from an exchange. Exchanges such as Coinbase, Quadriga, and Kraken.

Note: PixelProperty is not affiliated with any of the listed exchanges.

HOW DO I SEND ETH TO METAMASK?

Once you have acquired ETH on the exchange of your choice, you must send it to your MetaMask ETH address.

You can find your MetaMask ETH address in the MetaMask extension by clicking on the “...” menu button, then selecting “Copy To Clipboard”. Provide this copied address where requested to receive funds from a market or other address.

WHAT IS PIXELPROPERTY'S SMART CONTRACT ADDRESS

PixelProperty currently has two Ethereum Smart Contracts in the ecosystem, the PXLProperty contract and the DApp contract. PXLProperty is the ERC20 cryptocurrency and cryptocollectable smart contract, while the DApp holds the behaviour logic of the system.
