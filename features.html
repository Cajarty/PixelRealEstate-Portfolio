TECHNICAL DETAILS

  1. PixelProperty is an Ethereum Smart Contract powered by the Ethereum network
  2. PixelProperty is supported by a sustainable income model rather than an ICO

WEB CLIENT DETAILS

  1. PixelProperty.io uses Metamask, a browser plugin for your own private lite-wallet.
  2. With Metamask, your sensitive blockchain info is never shared with us.

PROPERTY DETAILS

  1. Each Digital Property is a unique, non-fungible token supported by Ethereum
  2. Users can set the display image, hover texts, and hyperlink of Properties
  3. Properties can be traded between users at any price, where a 2% fee will be charged to sustain monthly costs
  4. Owners of Properties may advertise or earn PXL by allowing the public to use their Property
  5. There are 10,000 unique Properties within the PixelProperty market. No new Properties can ever be created or destroyed
  6. Each Property will be available for purchase at launch in either PXL or ETH. These Properties are known as System Properties
  7. System-Properties can be purchased on a sliding scale between ETH and PXL, increasing independently after each sale
  8. The System-Property price is the going rate for all System-Properties. Buying one System-Property will increase the price for all other System-Properties
  9. System-Properties are converted into User-Properties once purchased
  10. User-Property sales in PixelProperty follow the free market. Similar to other collectables, the price of User-Properties are determined by free market supply and demand

PXL TOKEN DETAILS

  1. PixelPropertyToken(PXL) is the ERC20 cryptocurrency of PixelProperty
  2. PXL has no pre-mine. All coins are generated based on Property interaction
  3. PXL distribution fluctuates; the theoretical maximum is 2 PXL per Property per minute
  4. PXL used to update the canvas is burned. The more spent, the longer the reward payout and reservation time
  5. Properties using Private mode for advertising takes and burns one Token per minute from the user
